# QuillQuest — Support

**URL:** `/quillquest/support` · **App Store Connect:** Support URL (required)

QuillQuest is a free, offline spelling game for grades 3–8 (roughly ages 8–14). No accounts, no ads, no tracking.

## Need help?
Email **contact@malcom.io** and we'll get back to you.

## Common questions
- **Does it work offline?** Yes — fully offline, no internet needed to play.
- **Does it collect any data?** No. Progress, name, and avatar stay on the device. See the [Privacy Policy](/quillquest/privacy).
- **Can multiple kids share one device?** Yes — each kid gets their own profile, progress, streak, and avatar (Settings → Players).
- **How do I set it up?** Open the app, enter your child's age, and they're playing.
- **Is there a cost?** No — free, with no ads and no in-app purchases.
- **How do I reset progress?** Settings → Reset all progress.
