# QuillQuest 🪶

**URL:** `/quillquest` · **App Store Connect:** Marketing URL (optional)

### A spelling adventure that grows with your kid. Fun, not childish.

**Free · Offline · No ads · No tracking · No accounts.**

Hear a word, spell it out, climb the ranks. QuillQuest turns spelling into a quest — and builds real skill along the way. Great for grades 3–8 (roughly ages 8–14); it places every kid by level, so younger and older spellers fit right in too.

## Why parents trust it
- **Truly private** — plays fully offline, collects nothing, sends nothing anywhere. No ads, trackers, accounts, or chat.
- **Actually teaches** — a graded curriculum, mastery-based levels, and spaced-repetition review of the words your kid misses.
- **Healthy by design** — optional reminders (off by default), streaks that reward just showing up, no nagging.
- **For the whole family** — each kid gets their own profile, buddy, and avatar.

## What kids get
- 10 levels of hand-graded words (~3,500), from Sprout to the Bee Level
- Friendly characters (Ivy, Owen & Mia) read every word aloud in real recorded voices
- Miss a word? It shows the correct spelling and exactly where you slipped
- Ranks, stars, badges, and unlockable themes to earn
- Practice packs: compound words, homophones, prefixes & suffixes, and "words I missed"
